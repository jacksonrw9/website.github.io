Phizzle is a leader in intelligent manufacturing automation for regulated industries. Our Connected Plant platform digitizes GMP workflows by seamlessly connecting legacy analytical instruments to modern digital infrastructure without disrupting existing SOPs.

Our software-based solution automatically captures, formats, and syncs instrument data in real-time, eliminating pen-and-paper processes that compromise data integrity and slow manufacturing operations.

Our platform serves BioPharma, Food Production, and Aerospace manufacturers, scaling from single instruments to enterprise-wide deployments while maintaining the highest standards of compliance and audit readiness.
