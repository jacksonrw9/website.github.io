Connected Plant™

Automate Compliant, Error-Free Data Flow from Multi-Vendor Analytical Instruments to Existing LIMS, MES, or QMS

Enhance Validated Manufacturing Systems Without Middleware or Replatforming

Ensure Data Integrity

Eliminate Manual Errors in Scientific Workflows

  • Automate result capture, format standardization and contextualized data entry to existing Digital Systems
  • Schedule and initiate pre-configured samples from our vendor agnostic UI to reduce errors caused by gloves and front panel mistakes
  • Lock out instrument screens during sampling to avoid interference

Maintain Chain of Custody

Streamline Compliance

  • Capture Comprehensive Instrument, Operator and System Audit Logs
  • Query audit logs and sample results with Connected Plant™ and export the data as a CSV, Excel, JSON, or PDF file
  • Review, comment on, and e-sign sample results in accordance with 21 CFR Part 11

Accelerate Batch Release

Optimize Quality Review

  • Reduce time lag between Manufacturing and QA
  • Enable review by exception by flagging out of range readings in real time and notifying operators

Connected Plant™ Capabilities

Connect Analytical Scientific Instruments

Onboard multi-vendor instruments with our pre-built instrument connectors.

Enable Remote Sampling

Preconfigure workflow recipes on instruments that require manual intervention, or remotely run entire samples and avoid cleanroom entry for routine environmental monitoring.

Centralize Instrument Data and Audit Logs

Sample data and audit logs are stored on our platform and can be queried and exported.

Integrate with existing LIMS, MES, and QMS

Synchronize data directly with Digital Systems without altering infrastructure.

Support Paperless Scientific Workflows

Eliminate pen and clip board transcription and paper tape.

Unlock Multi-Directional Workflows With LIMS, MES and QMS

Run scheduled samples from LIMS and MES within our platform.
