Extend the Lifespan of Legacy Instruments

Rather than replacing your legacy instruments because of networking limitations or inadequate integration with modern digital systems, Phizzle's Climet 70 Series Connector allows regulated manufacturers to converts existing assets into connected, compliant nodes.

Bridge the Gap Between Multi-Vendor Instruments

Many regulated manufacturing facilities and R&D labs have 100s, if not 1000s of instruments, and often they are not standardized for one manufacturer per device class. This opens the door to error when operators have to follow different SOPs for each device to initiate samples and collect data.

With Phizzle's air particle counter connectors, operators can initiate samples from multi-vendor devices all from one view, eliminating:

  • Training on proprietary sampling methods between vendor
  • Glove-related and front-panel mistakes
  • Data formatting inconsistencies and integration challenges across vendor systems

Automate Capture of Far More Than Just Sample Data

Maintain comprehensive operational oversight and traceability by automatically capturing detailed instrument status updates, operator actions, and system events including:

  • Instrument offline updates, sample in-progress status and run start and stop times
  • Which operator started a sample, reviewed the results and synchronized data to the LIMS or ELN
  • When data was pushed to your backend systems or scheduled samples were pulled to be run on our UI

Streamline Compliance

Streamline review workflows by enabling operators to review, comment on, and electronically sign sample results in real-time in accordance with 21 CFR Part 11. Every action is time stamped and traceable, giving your QA teams full confidence in data integrity and compliance.
