Extend the Lifespan of Legacy Instruments

Building Towards Industry 4.0 Standards

Rather than replacing your legacy instruments because of networking limitations or inadequate integration with modern digital systems, Phizzle's Climet 50 Series Connector allows regulated manufacturers to modernize data collection practices with older existing instruments. This solution can either be the solution to analog data practices, or it can be the first step towards realizing Industry 4.0 Standards with the Phizzle Connector Plus.

Bridge the Gap Between Multi-Vendor Instruments

Many regulated manufacturing facilities and R&D labs have 100s, if not 1000s of instruments, and often they are not standardized for one manufacturer per device class. This opens the door to error when operators have to follow different SOPs for each device to initiate samples and collect data.

With Phizzle's air particle counter connectors, operators can initiate samples from multi-vendor devices all from one view, eliminating:

  • Training on proprietary sampling methods between vendor
  • Glove-related and front-panel mistakes
  • Data formatting inconsistencies and integration challenges across vendor systems

Automate Capture of Far More Than Just Sample Data

Maintain comprehensive operational oversight and traceability by automatically capturing detailed instrument status updates, operator actions, and system events including:

  • Instrument offline updates, sample in-progress status, and run start and stop times
  • Which operator started a sample, reviewed the results, and synchronized data to the LIMS or ELN
  • When data was pushed to your backend systems or scheduled samples were pulled to be run on our UI

Streamline Compliance

Streamline review workflows by enabling operators to review, comment on, and electronically sign sample results in real-time in accordance with 21 CFR Part 11. Every action is time stamped and traceable, giving your QA teams full confidence in data integrity and compliance. Enabling QA to:

  • Capture audit data that most systems miss
  • Easily query audit logs and sample results
  • Export data instantly to CSV, Excel, JSON, PDF

Frequently Asked Questions

Follow our simple 5 step onboarding process by scanning a QR code. Our engineering team handles all of the technical details, so it doesn't consume your IT resources to set up Climet instruments.

Samples can be initiated remotely through our web-based UI. This is also where sample results and audit logs are stored, and can be queried and downloaded as a CSV, Excel, JSON, or PDF.
