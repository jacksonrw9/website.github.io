Instrument Connectors

Modernize your regulated data workflows with solutions that fit with your existing infrastructure: From validated data extraction to fully automated, integrated processes. We offer different connectors to realize your specific digitization vision.

Different Connectors for Different Use Cases:

Data Connector: Eliminate Manual Data Capture

Securely capture data files from multi-vendor instruments without configuring each device to the network or burdening IT with updates and maintenance. Our data connectors collect files directly from an instrument and securely uploads the results to your backend servers. By simply connecting an instrument to our Edge Puck, you can:

  • Eliminate inefficient and error-prone manual data handling workflows
  • Collect validated results without exposing your instruments to direct network threats
  • Ensure chain of custody and compliance with automated audit logging for each result

Connector Plus: Unlock Industry 4.0 Standards

Unlike basic industry connectors, these connectors act as comprehensive device agents that handle everything behind the scenes: automatically communicating in the native protocols of each instrument, handling all of the idiosyncrasies between each device, and continuously tracks device updates with second-by-second HeartBeat monitoring. Connecting your instrument to a Connector Plus allows you to:

  • Automate error-free data capture, format standardization from multi-vendor instruments, and secure synchronization with existing backend systems (LIMS, ELN, QMS, etc.)
  • Initiate samples from your laptop and eliminate eliminate glove-related and front-panel mistakes
  • Enable review by exception workflows by notifying operators/reviews the moment a sample is out of specification
  • Collect detailed audit logs that include instrument activity, operator interactions, and system events to uphold chain of custody

Phizzle Edge Puck: The Shared Technical Foundation

The Edge Puck™ is a self-contained gateway with an integrated computer that hosts our instrument connectors directly on-device: Purpose-built for regulated manufacturing, cleanroom facilities, and laboratory environments, it is cleanable, portable, and fully compliant with cleanroom standards. This enables your team to:

  • Maintain continuous data capture even during network failures and power outages with battery-powered operation and private network architecture
  • Extend the lifespan of legacy instruments and avoid unnecessary hardware replacements
  • Secure vulnerable analytical instruments by converting exposed protocols like Modbus into encrypted MQTT communications, eliminating direct network access threats
